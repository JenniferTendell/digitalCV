Hi, I'm Jennifer!

I grew up in Lerum and moved to Halmstad 2015 to study Digital Design and Innovation. I’ve always had an interest for colors, shapes and creating things through my creative mind. When I was younger I used to create my own jewelry, nail art and paintings. Later on, I became more digital and continued my work in Illustrator and also got a greater interest in interior design.

After three years in Halmstad I moved to Göteborg and now I’m studying Front End Developing at Medieinstitutet. I thought it would be a great skill to have as a digital designer and now I really enjoy programming! I personally like structure and it’s fun to solve problems with creative solutions. I'm excited and motivated to learn more. If everything goes as planned, I can call myself a Front End Developer by the year 2022. To be continued...
