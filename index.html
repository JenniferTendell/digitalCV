About me

I’ve always had an interest for colors, shapes and creating things through my creative mind. As a little girl I used to make my own jewelry, nail art and paintings. Later on, I became more digital and continued my work in photoshop and illustrator. I love the process of putting things together to see how it can become something new. I’m a curious, calm and positive teamplayer that got an eye for details.

I decided to follow my interests and moved to Halmstad for three years to study Digital Design and Innovation. The education focused on user centered design and I learned to always see my design through different perspectives. I realized that design is so much more than just the look.

Now I’m eager to know how to make designs come to life! I’m on my way right now as a student at Medieinstitutet. If everything goes as planed, I can call myself a Front End Developer by the year 2022. To be continued...
